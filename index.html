How to pomodoro

  • Step 1: 🗒️ Pick a task
  • Step 2: ⏲️ Set timer for 25 min or 1 "Pomodoro"
  • Step 3: 💻 Work until timer rings
  • Step 4: ☕ Take a 5 min break
  • Step 5: 🔁 Repeat
  • Step 6: 🍽️ After 4 "Pomodoros", take a longer 30 min break
